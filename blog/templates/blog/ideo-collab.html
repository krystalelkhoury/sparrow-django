IDEO & Sparrow's Collaboration

By Deborah Hanus, Britteny Chieng
Date 2 March 2019
Share this post:

Last week, IDEO invited us to collaborate on new designs. We bundled in on a rainy day by the Bay Bridge on Embarcadero and sprinted some great ideas. We’re excited they love Sparrow and we can’t wait to show you what we worked on. Check out some photos from that day!

Sparrow's CEO, Deborah Hanus, unpacks the difficulties of managing employee leave, both for employer and the employee. Between managing the deadlines, the endorsements, the regulations, and filing nuances a companies spend 20-40 hours of employee time on managing a single leave!
The IDEO and Sparrow team get to action! We break down Sparrow’s three value propositions: saves time (20-40hr/leave), saves money ($20k/leave), and saves headache — and how to incorporate them in our new designs.
